Floating Around

Feathers often symbolize lightness, freedom, and the ability to rise above challenges. Their gentle movement reminds us that even the smallest things can drift, float, and find their place with ease. Just like a feather, we can move with the wind instead of fighting against it. Feathers also carry a sense of guidance, as if they appear in our path to remind us to trust the journey we’re on. Their softness shows that strength doesn’t always have to be loud or forceful — sometimes it’s the quiet things that lift us the highest.
